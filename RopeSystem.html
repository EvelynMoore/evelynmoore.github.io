Rope system

This is a system I made in Unity to simulate ropes. It is made of a list of rigidbodies drawn by a linerenderer. The rope can be wrapped around objects and then unwrapped without clipping into anything.

This system deals well with sharp corners on objects, as well as smooth objects such as spheres.

The rope also has gravity which can be enabled or disabled at will as well as rudimentary collision. I plan to add additional physics such as tension to this implementation as well.

The rope has several measures to maximise optimisation, for example vertices will get merged if they get too close together.
