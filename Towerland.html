Towerland Pitch Deck

Blender & Powerpoint
Design & Presentation
Uni Assignment
Solo Project

A pitch deck for a hypothetical mobile game I invented called Towerland, created for a university assignment. The deck can be found Here.

The game idea is an idle tower defence game where enemies approach even if you didn't have the game open, and your turrets will destroy them, getting you money and rewards even when you aren't actively playing. Special boss rounds would give you notifications so you can shore up your defences before the enemies arrive.

Creating and presenting a pitch deck was excellent practice for pitching ideas and games in the industry.

I used blender to make a small mockup of what the game would look like, as well as assets for use in the deck.

Players would also be able to take control of towers and fire them manually at enemies, which gives players something more active to do in the game if they are waiting for a timer or want to spend some time engaging more dynamically. It also provides a richness to the gameplay as there would be many different tower types and they would each feel different to use.
